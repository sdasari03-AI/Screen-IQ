Overview
SUMMARY

The Director of AI Agent Solution Consulting is a senior leader responsible for both people leadership and functional ownership of RealPage AI Agent implementations. This role oversees a high-performing team of seasoned consultants who deliver Subject Matter Expert (SME) insight to customers while serving as the primary Implementations representative and functional voice for AI Lumina Workforce products across the organization.

In addition to building and leading a results-driven consulting organization, the Director represents Implementations in cross-functional forums, including Weekly Business Reviews (WBRs), product alignment sessions, and executive stakeholder discussions with General Managers, Sales, Customer Success, and Product teams. This role ensures that implementation strategies, customer outcomes, and product direction are aligned with broader business goals.

The Director is accountable for implementation excellence, operational rigor, and customer success across AI Lumina Workforce products, ensuring scalable delivery, strong adoption outcomes, and continuous improvement.
